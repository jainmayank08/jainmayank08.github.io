About JES

Jain Engineers Society – Navi Mumbai was formed on 26th January 2013 in Pune during the Annual Convention of JES.

Vision

To enroll each and every Jain Engineer in Navi Mumbai and make them part of JES, so that they can share their knowledge and experience for the betterment of Jain Community and Society as a whole.
Objectives of Jain Engineers Society

Work for protection of environment, conservation of water and safeguarding of sources of energy in urban and rural regions. Work for conservation of heritage and archeology of ancient shrines, spreading knowledge about the scientific outlook and history of Jainism. Work for assistance and upliftment of people affected by natural calamities and social problems. Popularizing virtues like vegetarianism and non-violence. Guide young generation about career opportunities in the field of engineering and assist in providing employment opportunities to capable engineers. Conduct activities like organizing fairs, exhibitions and seminars for objectives such as Bringing Jain Engineers on a Forum for social and economic progress of the community, Providing incentives and scholarships for meritorious students in engineering and Facilitating contacts with established professionals in India and abroad.

Event & Programs

JES Navi Mumbai Chapter has several Events and Programs throughout the year. Every month there is an Executive Committee Members’ Meeting at any of the EC Member’s residence.

Members Dashboard

JES NaviMumbai offers Engineers several opportunities to bring out their hidden professional talents to contribute for the welfare of the Community.

Market Place

JES Market Place is an online Buyer-Seller meeting platform for buying any product or service – whether new or second or selling any surplus product or your service which your friends would be looking for.

Job Board

JES Job Board is an online platform for Job Seekers and Job Providers. You have to be JES Member to use this Job Board. Job Seekers – whether you are fresh Engineer of experienced, us this platform for your benefit. Job Providers – whether you need for your Company or any of your friends’ company, post your Job requirements here!
